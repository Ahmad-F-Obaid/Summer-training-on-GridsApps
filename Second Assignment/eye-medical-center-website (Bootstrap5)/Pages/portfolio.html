Pediatric Amblyopia Treatment

Pediatric Care

Patient: 6-year-old child

Condition: Severe amblyopia (lazy eye) in left eye

Treatment: Patching therapy combined with vision therapy exercises

Outcome: Vision improved from 20/200 to 20/30. Child now has functional binocular vision and improved depth perception.

Initial Vision: 20/200
Final Vision: 20/30
Treatment Duration: 8 months

Glaucoma Management

Glaucoma Treatment

Patient: 58-year-old business executive

Condition: Primary open-angle glaucoma with elevated IOP

Treatment: Combination medical therapy and selective laser trabeculoplasty

Outcome: IOP reduced from 28 mmHg to 14 mmHg. Visual field defects stabilized with no further progression.

Initial IOP: 28 mmHg
Current IOP: 14 mmHg
Follow-up Period: 3 years

Retinal Detachment Repair

Retinal Surgery

Patient: 45-year-old construction worker

Condition: Traumatic retinal detachment following workplace injury

Treatment: Emergency vitrectomy with gas tamponade

Outcome: Successful retinal reattachment with vision recovery from light perception to 20/40. Patient returned to work with protective eyewear.

Initial Vision: Light Perception
Final Vision: 20/40
Surgery Success: 100% Reattachment

Diabetic Retinopathy Treatment

Medical Retina

Patient: 62-year-old with Type 2 diabetes

Condition: Proliferative diabetic retinopathy with macular edema

Treatment: Anti-VEGF injections and panretinal photocoagulation

Outcome: Regression of neovascularization and resolution of macular edema. Vision stabilized at 20/30 with improved quality of life.

Initial Vision: 20/80
Current Vision: 20/30
Treatment Period: 18 months
