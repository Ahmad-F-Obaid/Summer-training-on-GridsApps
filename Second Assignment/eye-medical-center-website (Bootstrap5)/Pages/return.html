Return Policy

Our return and refund policy for eyewear, contact lenses, and other products and services.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Overview

At Eye Medical Center, we are committed to your satisfaction with our products and services. This Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returns, exchanges, and refunds for eyewear, contact lenses, and other products purchased from our practice.

Please note that medical services are generally non-refundable, but we will work with you to address any concerns about the quality of care you receive.

2. Eyewear Returns and Exchanges

2.1 Prescription Eyeglasses

Return Period: 30 days from the date of purchase

Conditions for Return:

  • Eyeglasses must be in original condition with no scratches or damage
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required
  • Prescription must be verified as accurate
  • Custom or specialty lenses may have different return policies

Refund Amount: Full refund minus any applicable restocking fees

2.2 Prescription Adjustments

If your prescription changes within 90 days of purchase due to an error in our examination or fitting:

  • We will remake your lenses at no charge
  • Frame exchange may be available if medically necessary
  • Verification of prescription change required

2.3 Sunglasses and Non-Prescription Eyewear

Return Period: 14 days from purchase

Conditions:

  • Items must be unworn and in original packaging
  • Tags and protective films must be intact
  • Original receipt required

2.4 Frame Warranty

We offer a one-year warranty on frame defects:

  • Manufacturing defects covered at no charge
  • Normal wear and tear not covered
  • Damage due to misuse or accidents not covered
  • Warranty replacement subject to availability

3. Contact Lens Returns

3.1 Unopened Contact Lenses

Return Period: 30 days from purchase

Conditions:

  • Packages must be unopened and sealed
  • Expiration date must be at least 6 months away
  • Original receipt required
  • Restocking fee may apply

3.2 Contact Lens Fitting Guarantee

For new contact lens wearers or those trying a new brand:

  • 90-day fitting guarantee period
  • Exchange for different brand/type if fitting issues occur
  • Follow-up appointments included in fitting fee
  • Unused lenses may be exchanged for different parameters

3.3 Specialty Contact Lenses

Custom or specialty contact lenses (scleral, toric, multifocal):

  • Extended fitting period up to 6 months
  • Multiple follow-up visits included
  • Lens modifications included in fitting fee
  • Non-refundable fitting fees for custom lenses

4. Medical Services

4.1 Examination Fees

Eye examination fees are generally non-refundable once services have been provided. However, we will address any concerns about the quality of your examination.

4.2 Surgical Procedures

Pre-Operative Cancellations:

  • Cancellations more than 48 hours before surgery: Full refund minus administrative fee
  • Cancellations within 48 hours: 50% refund
  • Same-day cancellations: No refund

Post-Operative Concerns:

  • Enhancement procedures included for qualifying patients
  • Complications covered under surgical warranty
  • Follow-up care included in surgical fee

4.3 Treatment Plans

For multi-visit treatment plans:

  • Refunds prorated based on services not yet provided
  • Administrative fees may apply
  • Medical necessity determines treatment modifications

5. Return Process

5.1 How to Initiate a Return

To return an item or request a refund:

  1. Contact our office at (123) 456-7890
  2. Provide your receipt or order information
  3. Explain the reason for return
  4. Schedule an appointment if evaluation is needed

5.2 Required Documentation

Please bring the following for all returns:

  • Original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Valid photo identification
  • Items in original packaging when applicable
  • Insurance information if applicable

5.3 Evaluation Process

Some returns may require evaluation by our staff:

  • Prescription verification for eyewear
  • Fit assessment for contact lenses
  • Damage inspection for warranty claims
  • Medical review for service-related concerns

6. Refund Methods and Timing

6.1 Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed using the original payment method:

  • Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days
  • Cash purchases: Immediate cash refund
  • Check payments: Refund check issued within 7 days
  • Insurance payments: Coordination with insurance provider

6.2 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may apply for:

  • Items returned outside the standard return period
  • Products showing signs of wear or use
  • Services partially completed
  • Administrative or restocking fees

6.3 Store Credit

In some cases, store credit may be offered:

  • Items returned without receipt
  • Exchanges for different products
  • Credit valid for one year from issue date
  • Non-transferable and non-refundable

7. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened contact lens packages (except under fitting guarantee)
  • Custom-made items that cannot be resold
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Products purchased more than one year ago
  • Gift certificates or store credit
  • Completed medical services

8. Insurance Considerations

8.1 Insurance-Covered Items

For items covered by insurance:

  • Returns may require insurance company approval
  • Refund amounts limited to patient responsibility
  • Insurance benefits may be affected by returns
  • Processing time may be extended

8.2 Warranty vs. Insurance

We will help determine whether issues are covered under:

  • Product warranty
  • Insurance benefits
  • Practice guarantee policies

9. Customer Satisfaction

9.1 Our Commitment

We are committed to your satisfaction and will work with you to resolve any issues with our products or services. Our goal is to ensure you receive the best possible eye care experience.

9.2 Problem Resolution

If you are not satisfied with a product or service:

  • Contact us immediately to discuss your concerns
  • We will work to find a mutually acceptable solution
  • Options may include repair, replacement, or refund
  • Patient care coordinator available to assist

10. Policy Changes

This Return Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on our website and in our office. The policy in effect at the time of purchase will govern your return rights.
