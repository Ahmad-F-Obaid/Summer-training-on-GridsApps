Our Services

Comprehensive eye care services using state-of-the-art technology and evidence-based treatments.

Comprehensive Eye Examinations

Our comprehensive eye examinations go beyond simple vision testing. We use advanced diagnostic equipment to evaluate your complete eye health and detect potential problems early.

What's Included:

  • Visual acuity testing
  • Refraction assessment
  • Eye muscle movement evaluation
  • Peripheral vision testing
  • Intraocular pressure measurement
  • Dilated fundus examination
  • Optical coherence tomography (OCT)
  • Digital retinal photography

Conditions We Screen For:

  • Glaucoma
  • Diabetic retinopathy
  • Macular degeneration
  • Cataracts
  • Retinal detachment
  • Dry eye syndrome

Advanced Eye Surgery

Our surgical team performs a wide range of eye surgeries using the latest techniques and technology to ensure optimal outcomes and patient safety.

Surgical Services:

  • Cataract Surgery: Premium lens implants, laser-assisted surgery
  • LASIK & PRK: Laser vision correction procedures
  • Glaucoma Surgery: Trabeculectomy, tube shunts, minimally invasive procedures
  • Retinal Surgery: Vitrectomy, retinal detachment repair
  • Corneal Procedures: Corneal transplants, cross-linking
  • Oculoplastic Surgery: Eyelid surgery, tear duct procedures

Advanced Technology:

  • Femtosecond laser systems
  • Phacoemulsification equipment
  • Vitrectomy machines
  • Excimer laser technology

Pediatric Eye Care

Children's eyes are constantly developing, making regular eye care essential. Our pediatric specialists provide comprehensive eye care designed specifically for infants, children, and teenagers.

Pediatric Services:

  • Infant eye screenings
  • School-age vision assessments
  • Amblyopia (lazy eye) treatment
  • Strabismus (crossed eyes) management
  • Pediatric glasses and contact lens fittings
  • Vision therapy programs
  • Learning-related vision problems

Age-Appropriate Care:

  • Infants (0-12 months): Basic eye health screening
  • Toddlers (1-3 years): Visual development assessment
  • Preschoolers (3-5 years): Pre-school vision screening
  • School-age (6+ years): Comprehensive eye exams

Contact Lens Services

Our contact lens specialists provide comprehensive fitting services and ongoing care to ensure optimal comfort and vision correction.

Contact Lens Types:

  • Soft Lenses: Daily, weekly, and monthly disposables
  • Rigid Gas Permeable: For complex prescriptions
  • Specialty Lenses: Toric, multifocal, and colored lenses
  • Scleral Lenses: For irregular corneas
  • Orthokeratology: Overnight corneal reshaping

Fitting Process:

  • Comprehensive eye examination
  • Corneal measurements and mapping
  • Trial lens fitting and evaluation
  • Insertion and removal training
  • Care and maintenance instruction
  • Follow-up appointments
