Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What should I bring to my pet's first visit?

Please bring any previous medical records, current medications, and a list of questions you may have about your pet's health.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including CareCredit and in-house payment plans for qualifying treatments.

How often should my pet have a checkup?

We recommend annual checkups for healthy adult pets and bi-annual visits for senior pets or those with chronic conditions.

Do you see exotic pets?

Yes, we have experience with birds, rabbits, guinea pigs, and other small animals. Please call to discuss your specific pet's needs.
