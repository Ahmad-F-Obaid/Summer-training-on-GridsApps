About PetCare Centre

Dedicated to providing exceptional pet care services with compassion, expertise, and a genuine love for animals since 2015.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, PetCare Centre began as a small veterinary clinic with a big vision: to provide comprehensive, compassionate care for pets and peace of mind for their owners.

What started as a single-room clinic has grown into a full-service pet care facility, serving thousands of happy pets and their families across our community. Our success is built on trust, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to animal welfare.

Today, we're proud to be the most trusted name in pet care, offering everything from routine checkups to emergency services, grooming, and boarding facilities.
