About Eye Medical Center

Dedicated to preserving and improving your vision with compassionate, comprehensive care.

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Eye Medical Center has been at the forefront of eye care innovation for over two decades. What started as a small practice with a vision to provide exceptional eye care has grown into a comprehensive medical center serving thousands of patients annually.

Our journey began with Dr. Sarah Mitchell, a board-certified ophthalmologist who recognized the need for advanced eye care services in our community. Today, we continue to honor that founding vision while embracing the latest technological advances in ophthalmology.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, comprehensive eye care services using state-of-the-art technology and evidence-based treatments, while maintaining the highest standards of patient care and safety. We are committed to preserving and improving our patients' vision and overall quality of life.

Our Vision

To be the leading eye care center in the region, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and compassionate patient care. We strive to set the standard for comprehensive eye health services and to be the trusted choice for patients and referring physicians.

Advanced Technology

At Eye Medical Center, we invest in the latest diagnostic and treatment technologies to ensure our patients receive the most advanced care available. Our state-of-the-art equipment includes:

  • Advanced OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) imaging systems
  • Femtosecond laser technology for precision surgery
  • Digital retinal photography and fluorescein angiography
  • Automated perimetry for visual field testing
  • Corneal topography and pachymetry systems
  • Premium intraocular lenses for cataract surgery
